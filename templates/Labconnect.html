About Us

LabConnect is a cutting-edge laboratory management system designed to streamline equipment borrowing and ensure a smooth experience for students and faculty. Our goal is to make laboratory resources accessible, efficient, and well-organized.

With LabConnect, you can easily request and track lab equipment, reducing downtime and improving learning experiences.
