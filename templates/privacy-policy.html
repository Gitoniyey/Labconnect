How to Reserve Equipment

Follow these steps to book equipment for use:

  1. Login to the System

    Click on Sign In and enter your email and password.

  2. Navigate to the Equipment Page

    Go to the Equipment List page and browse available equipment.

  3. Select Equipment

    Click on an item to check availability and details.

  4. Fill Out the Reservation Form

    Enter your student ID, purpose for borrowing, and preferred date/time.

  5. Submit Reservation Request

    Click Reserve Now and wait for confirmation via email.

  6. Pick Up the Equipment

    Once approved, visit the lab at the scheduled time with your student ID.

  7. Return the Equipment

    Ensure the item is returned in good condition before the due date.
